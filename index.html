Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SNAP food stamp benefits.

What are SNAP food stamp benefits?

SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program) provides monthly benefits to help low-income families purchase food. Benefits are loaded onto an EBT card that can be used at grocery stores and farmers markets to buy eligible food items.

How do I know if I'm eligible?

Eligibility is based on your household size, monthly income, and certain expenses. Generally, your household income must be at or below 130% of the federal poverty level. Our quick eligibility check can help determine if you qualify.

Is this service free?

Yes, our eligibility check is completely free. SNAP benefits themselves are free - there are no fees to apply or receive benefits.

How long does the application process take?

The initial eligibility check takes just 60 seconds. If you qualify, most states process applications within 30 days. In emergency situations, benefits can be approved within 7 days.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption to protect all your data. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.

What documents do I need?

You'll typically need proof of identity, Social Security numbers for household members, proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns), and proof of expenses like rent or utilities. Our system will guide you through exactly what's needed.
